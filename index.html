Nature

I take regular walks or jogs in the Wissahickon Valley Park and am just in love with that place. I used to try all different trails in Philly but now pretty much stick to the same ones, and I enjoy noticing how the same trails look different every time I'm there, even from one day to the next.

Cooking

I recently watched Green Book (2018) and in addition to it being my favorite movie I've seen this year, I could not get enough of the insanely charming food scenes. I immediately needed to cook spaghetti with (vegetarian) meatballs after watching it.

Travel

My friends and I traveled to Montreal in June and I've officially been converted from someone who didn't understand the hype around poutine to someone who cannot stop thinking about the delicious mushroom poutine I had from a food truck.
